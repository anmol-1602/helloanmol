### The Monthly Intent
Going into February, the goal was to eliminate "digital noise." I found myself checking metrics too often and building for the sake of completion rather than quality.

### Wins & Highlights
* **Deep Work:** Managed three 4-hour blocks of uninterrupted coding per week.
* **Health:** Consistently hit 8,000 steps despite the heavy workload.
* **Learning:** Finished reading *The Timeless Way of Building*.

### Challenges
The biggest hurdle was "context switching." I realized that moving between design tasks and engineering tasks in the same afternoon was killing my momentum. Next month, I'll try "day-theming."
